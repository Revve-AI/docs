The Live Calls board shows every AI voice call in progress for your team, updated in real time — who's calling, which agent is handling it, and how long it's been running. Open any call to listen in, take over with your own voice, or end it outright.

This is the voice equivalent of [Human Takeover](/inbox/human-takeover) in the chat Inbox — same idea, a supervisor stepping into a live conversation — but for phone calls instead of chat threads.

<Note>
Live Calls is a newly shipped feature and is still rolling out gradually. If you don't see **Live Calls** in your left nav, or don't see the **Live Observability** toggle on a voice agent's Security tab, it may not be enabled for your team or agent yet.
</Note>

## How it works

1. **Live Calls** appears in the left nav (Radio icon), between **Tickets** and **Campaigns**, at `/dashboard/{team}/live-calls`. It's gated by the same team permission as **History & Analytics** — there's no separate Live Calls permission, so anyone who can see call history can see the board.
2. A live-count badge next to the nav label shows how many calls are in progress right now.
3. Only calls from voice agents with **Live Observability** turned on appear on the board at all. This is a per-agent toggle on that agent's **Security** tab (see [Voice Agent Advanced Capabilities](/voice-agents/voice-agent-advanced-capabilities#security)), off by default — if an agent's calls never show up, check that setting first.
4. Clicking a row opens the call's detail page in a new browser tab, so the board keeps running in the background while you watch one call.
5. From the detail page, a floating control bar lets you listen in, take over, or end the call.
6. Taking over reconnects your browser with a new identity that has microphone and publish rights — the same mechanism used when a human accepts a warm/consult transfer (see [Transferring Calls to a Human](/voice-agents/call-transfer)). The AI goes silent immediately, and your speech starts appearing in the transcript as **Human Agent**.
7. Ending a call disconnects every participant on it immediately. There's no undo.

## The board

The header reads "Live Calls" with the subtitle "Every AI voice call in progress for this team, updated in real time." Below it, a stat strip shows "`{N}` calls live now" (singular: "1 call live now") and a "`{inbound}` inbound · `{outbound}` outbound" split, with a green pulsing dot whenever at least one call is live.

The table columns are: **Caller | Agent | Type | Direction | Duration | Status**.

When nothing is happening, the board shows a phone-off icon with "No calls in progress" instead of an empty table.

### Status badges

| Status | Label | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| `live` | **Live** (green, pulsing) | The call is in progress and the AI is still driving it. |
| `taken_over` | **Taken Over** (amber) | A supervisor has taken over — a human is on the call instead of the AI. |
| `stale` | **Stale** (gray) | The board lost track of the call's live state, usually because a webhook update was missed. |
| `ended` | **Ended** (gray) | The call has finished. |

## The call detail view

Clicking a row opens `/dashboard/{team}/live-calls/{room}` in a new tab. The header has a back arrow to the board, the caller's name or number, direction, and status badge, with a "`{agent name}` · `{duration}`" subtitle underneath. A **View full** link opens the call's existing Call History detail page in yet another new tab — useful once the call ends and you want the full post-call record.

An info rail alongside the transcript mirrors the shape of the existing Call Detail info panel:

- **Call Info** — Type, Direction, Agent, Hotline, and "Started `{duration}` ago".
- **Contact** — Name and Phone.

The live transcript shows speaker bubbles labeled **Caller**, **Assistant**, or **Human Agent** — the **Human Agent** label only appears once a supervisor has taken over, or during a warm/consult transfer.

A **Summary** panel refreshes roughly every 30 seconds. Before any AI summary exists, it shows the warm-transfer handoff summary captioned "Handoff summary from the assistant" if the call arrived via a transfer. Once a summary has been generated, the panel instead shows "`{time}` ago". If there's no transfer and no summary yet, it reads "Summary appears once the call gets going."

## Listening in, taking over, or ending a call

A floating control bar on the detail page changes shape depending on whether you've taken over.

### Before you take over

- **Listen In** toggles to **Listening** (a pressed, amber state) once active — it streams the live call audio to you so you can hear the conversation as it happens.
- **Take Over** opens a confirm dialog: "Take over this call?" — "You'll join as a live participant with your microphone on, replacing the assistant for the rest of the call. The caller will hear you." — **Cancel** / **Take Over**.
- **End Call** (destructive) opens a confirm dialog: "End this call?" — "This disconnects everyone on the call immediately. This can't be undone." — **Cancel** / **End Call**.

<Note>
Listening in is silent to everyone else on the call — there's currently no indicator to the caller or the agent that a supervisor is listening in. Keep that in mind when you set expectations with your team about monitoring live calls.
</Note>

### After you take over

- **Mute** / **Unmute** — toggles your microphone.
- **Hang Up** (destructive) — disconnects every participant immediately, same as **End Call**.

## Who can do what

Two separate things are gated here, and it's easy to mix them up:

| Right | Gated by | Scope |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Seeing a call on the board, listening in, and watching its transcript | **Live Observability**, a per-voice-agent toggle on that agent's **Security** tab (off by default). Description in the app: "Allow team members to watch, listen to, and take over this agent's calls from the Live Calls board." | Per agent — if it's off, that agent's calls don't show up on the board at all. |
| Taking over or ending a call already on the board | Team access to the Live Calls board itself (the same permission as History & Analytics) | Team-wide — any team member who can see the board can take over or end any call listed on it. |

In practice: turn on **Live Observability** for each voice agent you want to monitor (see [Voice Agent Advanced Capabilities](/voice-agents/voice-agent-advanced-capabilities#security)) before expecting to see its calls at all. Once a call is visible, any team member with board access can act on it — there's no additional per-call approval step.

## When to use it

| Scenario | What to do |
|----------|-------------|
| Spot-checking call quality | Open a live call and **Listen In** without interrupting it. |
| A caller is escalating, frustrated, or the AI is stuck | **Take Over** — you join with your microphone on and the assistant steps aside. |
| A call has gone wrong and needs to stop immediately (compliance risk, abusive caller) | **End Call** or **Hang Up** to disconnect everyone right away. |
| You want the full post-call record after the call ends | Click **View full** from the call's detail page to open it in Call History. |
