# Monitoring Campaigns

![Campaign dashboard showing enrollment metrics, status breakdown, and exit outcomes](/screenshots/campaign-dashboard.png)

Once a campaign is running, you need to track its progress, catch issues early, and understand how leads are moving through the engagement flow.

## Campaign Dashboard

Open any campaign from the **Campaigns** list to see its dashboard. The dashboard shows:

| Metric | What it tells you |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Total Enrollments** | How many leads have been added to this campaign. |
| **Status Breakdown** | How many are active, paused, completed (success), or completed (failure). |
| **Call / Message Volume** | Total outbound actions taken by the campaign's agents. |

Use the date range filter in the top-right to scope metrics to a specific period.

## Enrollment Detail

Click any enrollment row to see the full journey of a single lead:

- **Engagement Timeline** — a chronological log of every action taken: calls made, messages sent, AI decisions, state transitions, and exit.
- **AI Decision Log** — the reasoning behind each action the AI engine took. Useful for debugging unexpected behavior.
- **Contact Information** — the lead's profile and any fields collected during interactions.
- **Sessions** — detailed records of each call or conversation, including transcripts, duration, sentiment, and outcome.

## Call History

For a broader view across all campaigns, use **History → Call History** in the sidebar. You can filter by:

- Campaign name
- Date range
- Call status (processed, failed, in progress)
- Direction (inbound, outbound)
- Disconnection reason
- Sentiment

Click any row to open the full call detail with audio recording, transcript, tool calls, and analysis.

## What to watch after launching a campaign

| When | What to check | Why |
|------|---------------|-----|
| **First 15 minutes** | Dashboard — are enrollments progressing from Active? | Confirms the AI engine is scheduling actions. |
| **First 30 minutes** | Call History — are calls connecting? | Catches telephony or number issues early. |
| **First hour** | Enrollment detail — sample 3–5 completed enrollments | Confirms the full flow works: call → collect data → exit. |
| **First day** | Dashboard — status breakdown | Check the success/failure ratio. High failure rate may indicate bad exit conditions or prehook misconfiguration. |
| **Ongoing** | Paused enrollments | If many enrollments are paused, the prehook API may be failing. |

## Exporting data

Click **Export** on the campaign dashboard or on the Call History page to download data as CSV. Use this for:

- Offline QA review
- BI and reporting tools
- Sharing results with stakeholders
